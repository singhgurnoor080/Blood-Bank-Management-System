ABOUT THE BLOOD BANK

Nature of the Organization: It is totally non-political, non-communal, non-regional, non-racial, secular and voluntary social organization. Goals and objectives: Initiating social movement to build up a healthy society through motivating voluntary blood donation, donating blood voluntarily and others services and awareness programs. Programs: Motivating Students and Young generation of Bangladesh to donate blood voluntarily. Free blood group test. Donating blood voluntarily for patients. Motivating people for voluntary blood donation and taking part in service and awareness program. Taking relief and rehabilitation programs to overcome natural and human created calamities.

"Blood Buddies is for public donationcenter with blood donation members in the changing health care system. - Specialist blood donors and clinical supervision. - Increasing communication with our members. - High quality assessment, diagnosis and treatment. - Examine critically to ensure alignment. - The extra care of a multi-disciplinary team.
